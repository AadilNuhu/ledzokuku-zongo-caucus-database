My Process

1. Discovery & Planning

The first step in any project is to understand your needs and objectives. I conduct thorough research and gather requirements to ensure a clear understanding of your goals. This phase includes defining project scope, target audience, and key features.

2. Design

Next, I create design mockups and wireframes to visualize the project's look and feel. This phase involves developing a user-friendly interface that aligns with your brand and enhances the user experience. I use tools like Adobe XD and Figma to design engaging and responsive layouts.

3. Development

Once the design is finalized, I move on to development. This involves coding the front-end and back-end of the website or application, ensuring that all features work seamlessly and that the site is optimized for performance and accessibility.

4. Testing & Launch

Before launching, I conduct rigorous testing to identify and fix any issues. This includes checking for bugs, ensuring cross-browser compatibility, and verifying that the site performs well on various devices. After thorough testing, I prepare for a smooth launch.

5. Maintenance & Support

Post-launch, I offer ongoing maintenance and support to address any issues that may arise. This includes updating software, fixing bugs, and making improvements based on user feedback to ensure your site remains up-to-date and functional.
